# Privacy Policy for Zakker (ذكّر)

**Last Updated:** June 14, 2026

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the "Zakker" Chrome extension handles information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
* **No Data Collection:** The Zakker extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, sensitive user information, browsing history, or user activity.
* **Local Storage:** All settings and configurations (such as notification intervals, language settings, and display mode preferences) are stored strictly locally on your device via the standard Google Chrome extension storage API (`chrome.storage.sync`). No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

## 2. Permissions Used
* **Alarms:** Used strictly to schedule and trigger periodic reminders locally on your device.
* **Notifications:** Used strictly to show local system notifications when Chrome is minimized or in the background.
* **Storage:** Used to save your settings preferences locally on your browser.

## 3. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
